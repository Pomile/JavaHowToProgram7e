This CD-ROM contains several Additional References not included in the book. These are chapters and appendices from other Deitel® titles and are being provided for reference purposes. They are in Adobe Acrobat PDF format. You can view or print them using Adobe® Acrobat® Reader®, or, if you have the PDF plug-in installed for your browser, you can view or print them by clicking on the links below. Some of the Additional References have example files.

The Additional References are located within the 'additional\addnlRefs' directory on the root level of this CD.

Note: Windows users, after you copy the example folders from a CD-ROM to your computer, the folders and their contents may display "Read-only" attributes that prevent you from editing them. If this occurs, follow these steps:

  1. Using Windows Explorer or My Computer navigate to the folder that you copied to your harddrive.
  2. Right-click on the folder icon and choose Properties.
  3. Clear the Read-only checkbox, and click OK.
